Casey Hunt

Hi, I’m Casey (she/her)! I am a second year PhD Student at the THING Lab in the ATLAS Institute. I design and build networked tangible and embodied interaction systems. In my work, I explore how we can better create and connect using systems that de-center or supplement screens. In my work, I seek to engage the wider community about how to make effective 3-D interfaces, so I focus on participatory design practices and toolkit design.
